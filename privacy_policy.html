1. Introduction

SCS Tech ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Mumbai Fire Brigade Case Monitoring mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using our App, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Information

Our App collects precise location data to provide emergency response services:

  • Real-time GPS coordinates - Latitude and longitude for vehicle tracking
  • Location accuracy data - GPS satellite count and signal strength
  • Movement data - Speed, direction, and route information
  • Background location - Continuous tracking for emergency response

2.2 Device Information

We collect device-specific information for system optimization:

  • Device model and operating system
  • Battery level and charging status
  • Network connectivity information
  • App usage statistics

2.3 User Account Information

  • Vehicle ID and assignment information
  • User identification for emergency personnel
  • Contact information for emergency coordination

3. How We Use Your Information

Data Type Purpose Legal Basis
Location Data Emergency vehicle tracking, incident response, route optimization Public safety, emergency services
Device Information App optimization, battery management, connectivity monitoring Legitimate interest
User Account Data Personnel identification, emergency coordination Contractual necessity
Usage Analytics App improvement, performance monitoring Legitimate interest

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Emergency Services

We share location and operational data with:

  • Mumbai Fire Brigade command centers
  • Emergency response coordination systems
  • Public safety agencies during emergencies
  • Incident management systems

4.2 Third-Party Services

We may share data with trusted third parties for:

  • Map and navigation services (Ola Maps)
  • Cloud storage and backup services
  • Analytics and monitoring tools
  • Emergency communication systems

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect public safety
  • Respond to emergency situations
  • Investigate security incidents

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication
  • Secure Servers: Data stored on secure, monitored servers
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Incident Response: Rapid response to security threats

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for the following periods:

  • Location Data: 30 days for operational purposes, 7 years for legal compliance
  • Account Information: Duration of employment plus 7 years
  • Emergency Records: 10 years for incident documentation
  • Analytics Data: 2 years for app improvement

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Location Permissions

You can control location access through your device settings:

  • Grant or revoke location permissions
  • Choose between "While Using App" or "Always"
  • Note: Emergency services require "Always" permission

7.2 Data Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request data portability
  • Object to certain data processing

7.3 Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data, subject to:

  • Legal retention requirements
  • Emergency response obligations
  • Public safety considerations

8. Children's Privacy
