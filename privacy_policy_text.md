# Privacy Policy - Mumbai Fire Brigade Case Monitoring

**Last Updated:** December 2024  
**Effective Date:** December 2024

## 1. Introduction

SCS Tech operates the Mumbai Fire Brigade Case Monitoring mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our emergency response app.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Location Data (Essential for Emergency Services)
- **Real-time GPS coordinates** for vehicle tracking
- **Location accuracy data** including GPS satellite count
- **Movement data** such as speed and direction
- **Background location** for continuous emergency tracking

### Device Information
- Device model and operating system
- Battery level and charging status
- Network connectivity information
- App usage statistics

### User Account Information
- Vehicle ID and assignment details
- Emergency personnel identification
- Contact information for coordination

## 3. How We Use Your Information

| Data Type | Purpose | Legal Basis |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Location Data | Emergency vehicle tracking, incident response, route optimization | Public safety, emergency services |
| Device Information | App optimization, battery management | Legitimate interest |
| User Account Data | Personnel identification, emergency coordination | Contractual necessity |
| Usage Analytics | App improvement, performance monitoring | Legitimate interest |

## 4. Data Sharing

We share data with:
- **Mumbai Fire Brigade command centers**
- **Emergency response coordination systems**
- **Public safety agencies during emergencies**
- **Map and navigation services (Ola Maps)**
- **Cloud storage and backup services**

## 5. Data Security

We protect your information through:
- **Encryption** of all data in transit and at rest
- **Access controls** and authentication
- **Secure servers** with monitoring
- **Regular security audits**
- **Incident response procedures**

## 6. Data Retention

- **Location Data:** 30 days operational, 7 years legal compliance
- **Account Information:** Duration of employment + 7 years
- **Emergency Records:** 10 years for incident documentation
- **Analytics Data:** 2 years for app improvement

## 7. Your Rights

You can:
- Control location permissions through device settings
- Access and correct your personal data
- Request data portability
- Object to certain data processing
- Request data deletion (subject to legal requirements)

## 8. Emergency Services Notice

⚠️ **Important:** This app is designed for emergency response. Location data collection is essential for public safety and emergency coordination. By using this app, you acknowledge that location tracking is necessary for emergency response operations.
